FAQ

How do I add a Builder?
If a builder is missing from the list, we'd love for you to add it to our database. To add a builder, first be sure you're viewing the city that the builder is located in. Click Add Builder, located in the navigation bar at the bottom of each page. On the Add a Builder page, please enter as much information as you can about the builder. When you are finished, click Add Builder.
Where is the Builder I added?
We review all new entries before they are added. It is not an automated process, so it may take a little time.
